The butterfly game is a game in which a small boy catches butterflies. As the boy catches more butterflies, he has a time limit for catching them with a challenge of staying away from the wasps he is allergic too. He kills wasps be smacking them with his net.As he catches more, the time will extend but more butterflies need to be caught or the game will end. If player gets at last level of catching all butterflies in time limit, the small boy will need to kill a queen wasp boss.
